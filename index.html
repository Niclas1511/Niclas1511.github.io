Niclas Niederdrenk

About me

My name is Niclas Niederdrenk and I am a PhD student under the supervision of Thomas Kahle at Otto von Guericke University Magdeburg. My research interests lie in commutative algebra, computational algebra, and combinatorics, with a particular focus on monomial ideals, binomial ideals, and free resolutions, as well as the computer algebra system Macaulay2.
